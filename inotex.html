INOTEX(Nutex)

INOTEX(Nutex) Is a Copolymer Emulsion based decorative paint, It containing quartz fine aggregate giving a light/medium/heavy textured, It is weather resistant, flexible decorative finish. It contains antifungal and antibacterial additives.

Instruction for use

All surfaces to be applied should be sound and thoroughly clean. Remove all dust, dirt, rust, oil, grease and any loose particle, which will affect the adhesion. Make sure that surface is clean and free from standing water.

Storage : Should be stored in warehouse condition, keep away from moisture and humidity.

Shelf Life : 12 month from the date of manufacture in original packing, if stored properly.

Packing : 30 kg Pail.
